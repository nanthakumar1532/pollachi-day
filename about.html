Welcome to Pollachi Day!

Pollachi Day is a celebration of the rich culture, heritage, and vibrant community of Pollachi, a picturesque town nestled in the foothills of the Western Ghats in Tamil Nadu, India. Our mission is to bring together residents and visitors alike to celebrate the unique traditions, delicious cuisine, and colorful festivals that make Pollachi a special place.

Fostering Unity and Innovation for Bright Future!

At Pollachi Day, we believe in fostering community spirit and pride. Our vision is to create a platform where people can come together to share stories, experiences, and laughter. We aim to promote local talent, arts, and crafts while encouraging sustainable practices that preserve our environment for future generations.
